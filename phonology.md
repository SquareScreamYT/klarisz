## Phonology

### Consonants

| Manner/Place | Bilabial | Alveolar | Palatal  | Velar  |
|--------------|----------|----------|----------|--------|
| **Nasal**    | m        | n        | ɲ        | ŋ      |
| **Plosive**  | p, b     | t, d     | ɟ        | k, g   |
| **Fricative**| ɸ~f, β\~v| s, z     | ç        | x\~h   |
| **Liquid**   |          | ɾ\~l\~r  | j        | w      |

### Vowels

|           | Front | Back |
|-----------|-------|------|
| **Close** | i     | u    |
| **Mid**   | e~ə   | o    |
| **Open**  | a     |      |

---

### Phonotactic Rules:

- **ŋ** cannot be an onset.
- **x** cannot be a coda.
- **e** at the end of a word will become **ə**.
