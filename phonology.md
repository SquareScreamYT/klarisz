---

### Phonotactic Rules:

- **c** can only be followed by **l**, **w**, **y**, or **r** as onset.
- **c** can be preceded by any sound except fricatives.
- **ŋ** is an allophone of **n** before **g** or **k**.
- **h** cannot be a coda.
